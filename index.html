About

The Downtown Benny Brown Show is a music radio show for independent music artists. It is a platform for independent music artist to be heard. Submit your music to bennybrown6278@gmail.com The country music show is every Wednesday. The Chicano gangster rap show is every Friday and the hip hop and R & B show is every Saturday. You will also find funny skits, comedy commercials and other fun stuff in the SKIT section of the page.
